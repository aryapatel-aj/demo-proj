REFUND POLICY

At SHINELAB, we strive to provide you with the highest quality jewelry and exceptional customer service. We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase, and we understand that sometimes returns are necessary. Please read our return policy carefully to ensure a smooth and hassle-free experience.

Eligibility for Returns:
  • We accept returns within 30 days from the date of delivery.
  • The item must be in its original condition, unworn, and with all original tags and packaging intact.
  • Personalized or custom-made jewelry cannot be returned unless it is defective or damaged upon arrival.
Eligibility for Returns:
  1. We accept returns within 30 days from the date of delivery.
  2. The item must be in its original condition, unworn, and with all original tags and packaging intact.
  3. Personalized or custom-made jewelry cannot be returned unless it is defective or damaged upon arrival.
Return Process:
  • To initiate a return, please contact our customer support team within the 30-day return period.
  • Our team will guide you through the return process, provide you with a return authorization number (RMA), and detailed instructions on how to return the item.
  • Please ensure that the item is securely packaged to prevent any damage during transit.
Return Shipping:
  • The customer is responsible for the return shipping costs, unless the item is defective, damaged, or there was an error on our part.
  • We recommend using a trackable shipping method and purchasing shipping insurance to protect the value of the item being returned.
Refund Process:
  • Once we receive the returned item and verify its condition, we will process the refund to the original payment method used for the purchase.
  • Refunds will be issued within 10 business days from the date of receiving the returned item.
  • Please note that the original shipping charges are non-refundable.
Exchanges:
  • We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you wish to exchange an item, you will need to follow the return process and place a new order for the desired item.
Damaged or Defective Items:
  • In the rare event that your item arrives damaged or defective, please contact our customer support team immediately.
  • We will provide instructions on how to return the item, and we will cover the return shipping costs.
  • Once we receive the damaged or defective item, we will either replace it with a new one or provide a full refund, including shipping charges.

Please note that this return policy applies only to purchases made directly from SHINELAB. If you purchased our products through authorized retailers or third-party websites, their return policies will govern the returns.
We are committed to ensuring your satisfaction and will do our best to resolve any issues promptly and fairly. If you have any further questions or need assistance, please don't hesitate to contact our friendly customer support team.
